Payslip Generator

About the App

This app generates a payslip based on the provided information. It calculates the monthly salary, professional tax, health insurance, and net salary. The app also includes details about earnings and deductions.

Calculations

  • Monthly Salary: Annual income divided by 12.
  • Professional Tax: Calculated based on the current tax slab.
  • Health Insurance: 2% of the monthly salary.
  • Net Salary: Monthly salary minus professional tax and health insurance.
  • Basic Salary: 50% of the monthly salary.

  • Adhoc Allowance: 15% of the monthly salary.
  • Miscellaneous Allowance: 5% of the monthly salary.
  • Gross Earnings: Sum of basic salary, HRA, adhoc allowance, and miscellaneous allowance.
